Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our delivery services.

How fast is your delivery service?
Our average delivery time in Nairobi is under 30 minutes. However, delivery times may vary depending on traffic conditions, restaurant preparation time, and your location.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve all major neighborhoods in Nairobi including Westlands, Kilimani, Karen, Lavington, and the CBD. We're continuously expanding to new areas.
How do I track my delivery?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS or in the app. You can see your rider's location in real-time and get estimated arrival times.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery for your convenience. All online payments are secured with encryption.

About IPATE Deliveries

Learn more about our mission, values, and the team behind Nairobi's fastest growing delivery service.

Our Story

IPATE Deliveries was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make life easier for Nairobi residents by providing fast, reliable, and affordable delivery services.

We noticed that despite the growing demand for delivery services in Nairobi, many existing options were either too slow, unreliable, or expensive. We set out to change that by building a technology-driven platform that connects customers with verified riders and local businesses.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across Nairobi and have built partnerships with over 200 local restaurants and stores. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To revolutionize delivery services in Nairobi through technology, efficiency, and exceptional customer experience.

Our Vision

To become Nairobi's most trusted and preferred delivery service, known for reliability and innovation.

Our Values

Customer-first approach, transparency, innovation, and community support guide our operations.

  • With Service Providers: We share information with trusted third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf.
