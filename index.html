Frequently Asked Questions

How fast are your deliveries?
Our average delivery time in Nairobi is 30 minutes. Delivery times may vary based on distance, traffic, and order complexity.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve all major areas in Nairobi including CBD, Westlands, Kilimani, Lavington, Karen, and surrounding neighborhoods.
How does the verification process work?
Our verification includes document validation (KRA PIN, National ID), background checks, and in some cases physical verification to ensure safety and reliability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-PESA, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery for certain orders. All digital payments are secure and encrypted.

About IPATE

IPATE is Nairobi's fastest-growing delivery platform, connecting customers, sellers, and riders through cutting-edge technology and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To revolutionize urban logistics in Kenya by providing fast, reliable, and affordable delivery services while creating economic opportunities for riders and sellers.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading delivery platform, known for innovation, reliability, and community impact.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We deliver on our promises, every time
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our technology and services
  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our customers, riders, and partners
  • Transparency: We believe in open communication and honest pricing
  • Community: We're building a platform that benefits everyone involved

Our Story

Founded in 2023, IPATE was born from the need for a more reliable, transparent, and efficient delivery service in Nairobi. Our founders, experienced in both technology and logistics, saw an opportunity to create a platform that would benefit customers, businesses, and delivery riders alike.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers and partner with hundreds of businesses across Nairobi, with plans to expand to other major Kenyan cities in the near future.
